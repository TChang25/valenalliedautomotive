Our Story

Serving West Palm Beach with mechanical excellence since 2020.

Family Owned & Operated

Founded by Joseph Li, Valen Allied Automotive was established with a mission to provide honest, dealership-quality service at unbeatable prices.

Since opening our doors in 2020, we have built a reputation for solving the most complex mechanical challenges. We specialize in the unique needs of Foreign, Domestic, and Japanese vehicles, ensuring that every car that enters our shop receives precision care tailored to its manufacturer specifications.

As a family-owned business, we prioritize transparency and long-term relationships with our clients. Whether you are coming in for a routine check-up or a major overhaul, you can trust that your vehicle is in the hands of experts who care about your safety and satisfaction.

Advanced Diagnostics

We utilize the latest technology to accurately identify issues with check engine lights, complex electrical systems, and overall vehicle drivability.

Full-Scale Mechanical

Our team is highly skilled in major labor, including engine and transmission replacements, suspension tuning, and air conditioning repair.

Japanese Auto Experts

With deep expertise in Japanese imports, we offer specialized maintenance that preserves the performance and longevity of your specific make and model.
