Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

At Valen Allied Automotive, we collect information necessary to provide high-quality automotive repair services. This includes:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and email address.
  • Vehicle Information: VIN, make, model, year, and service history.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used specifically for:

  • Scheduling appointments and providing repair estimates.
  • Communicating updates regarding your vehicle's service status.
  • Maintaining service records for your Japanese, Foreign, or Domestic vehicle.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website or servicing your vehicle (such as parts suppliers), so long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

4. Security of Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your details via our contact forms.
